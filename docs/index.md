# Hellbreakers — Player Wiki

Welcome to the Hellbreakers campaign wiki. The Hellbreakers League fights to free Isger from Chelish rule, and you have thrown in with them. This site collects the story so far, per-session recaps, an interlinked lore codex, and world background — everything the party has learned, and nothing it hasn't.
