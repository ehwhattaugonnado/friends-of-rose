# Last Time On — Hellbreakers

Dramatic "previously on" recaps, newest first. One short entry (2–4 sentences) prepended per completed session. Player-known information only.

## Session 002

Following the trail to the docks, the party buys a street urchin's whisper and forces the warehouse door — straight into a falling-crate trap that nearly kills Yun Ze on the spot. Inside, Chelaxian loyalists have Gertraud Mildenhall bound and waiting; the fight is desperate, Edlo dropping twice before Korrik carves the captors apart and Yun Ze cuts the smuggler free. But the prize is hollow: there were never any supplies on the ship, and no one can say how the enemy found a hidden warehouse. Patched up and paid out at the Fortnight Inn, the party takes Rhokrag's next charge — a little piracy on the river.

## Session 001

In the shadow of the Battle of Hellknight Hill, four strangers throw in with the injured Eagle Knight Ethran Howell and ride for Saringallow — only to be ambushed by undead bats at a fog-choked river crossing. Bloodied but unbroken, they reach the Hellbreakers' hidden headquarters, where the stern lieutenant Rhokrag hands them their first charge: the smuggler Gertraud Mildenhall has vanished at the Everbloom Imports warehouse, and the docks are no longer safe.
