# Running Log — Hellbreakers

## Open Quests

- Ambush a Chelaxian-flagged cargo ship up-river and bring it to the warehouse

## Session 001

- Recruited into the Hellbreakers by Ethran Howell in Breachill after the Battle of Hellknight Hill.
- Traveled to Saringallow, destroying undead bats at a river crossing on the way (Korrik Vesh briefly knocked unconscious).
- Reached the Hellbreakers' Saringallow headquarters and met the lieutenants — Maire Phelan, Rhokrag, and Xerra Bluebottle.
- Took on the league's first mission: investigate Everbloom Imports, find the missing Gertraud Mildenhall, and preserve the Logas supplies.

## Session 002

- Bribed a street urchin near the docks for intel: armed men had forced the warehouse that morning, and a halfling woman with a key went in earlier and never came out.
- Forced the warehouse side door and triggered a falling-crate trap (Yun Ze briefly knocked unconscious).
- Fought and killed the Chelaxian loyalists (a leader and three others) holding the warehouse; Edlo was downed twice, the rest were badly hurt, but all survived.
- Rescued Gertraud Mildenhall and learned the expected supply shipment was never on the ship.
- Acquired two magic trinkets from Gertraud — the Whispering Wire and the Masquerade Scarf (Tainis took the scarf) — plus a heavy crossbow and bolts; donated spare weapons and armor to the League.
- Reported back to Rhokrag; no one could explain how the loyalists found the hidden warehouse. Rested at the Fortnight Inn.
- Accepted Rhokrag's next mission: ambush a Chelaxian-flagged cargo ship up-river and bring it to the warehouse, keeping killing to a minimum. (Tainis Falco is now playing a bard.)
