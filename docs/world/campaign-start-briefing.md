# Campaign Start Briefing

_Player-safe handout for characters at the start of Chapter 1. Pair this with one of the `history-of-the-conflict` summaries if you want more background on the wider war._

## The Short Version

You begin this campaign as 1st-level, low-ranking members of the Hellbreakers League. You are not the movement's famous leaders or master spies. You are trusted enough to take assignments, useful enough to be worth sending into danger, and committed enough to have chosen a side in a country that is sliding toward open war.

At the start of play, Breachill has just been freed from a brutal Hellknight occupation. The Hellbreakers and Andoran's Eagle Knights helped win that victory at the Battle of Hellknight Hill, but nobody believes the danger is over. Cheliax still rules Isger, the government in Elidir still serves Chelish interests, and the wider conflict between Cheliax and Andoran is getting hotter.

## What Your Character Already Knows

- Isger is a vassal state under Cheliax, and many people believe Cheliax cares more about order, trade, and obedience than about ordinary Isgeri lives.
- The Goblinblood Wars still define daily life in Isger. Many people lost family, homes, or livelihoods, and the countryside still carries those scars.
- The Hellbreakers League is a rebel movement that wants to free Isger from Chelish rule. Its values center on resisting tyranny, protecting innocents, and rejecting self-serving cruelty.
- The Order of the Rack occupied Breachill while hunting the Hellbreakers and rumored warshards. During that occupation, the Hellknights used fear, executions, and public repression.
- Retired Andoren general Reginald Cormoth came to Breachill as part of a diplomatic effort and was publicly executed by the Hellknights.
- Eagle Knights and Hellbreakers later drove the occupiers out in the Battle of Hellknight Hill, despite the Hellknights receiving aid from a diabolic dragon.
- That victory gave people hope, but it also made open war between Cheliax and Andoran feel much more real.
- Loyalty in Isger is divided. Some people hate Cheliax, some fear rebellion, and some remain grateful for the aid Cheliax and the Church of Asmodeus provided after the Goblinblood Wars.

## Where the Campaign Opens

- You are gathering in Breachill after the Battle of Hellknight Hill.
- Your immediate contact is Ethan Howell, a wounded Eagle Knight veteran who wants to help the Hellbreakers and travel with you to Saringallow.
- Saringallow is the headquarters of the Hellbreakers League and the place you are headed next.
- The opening atmosphere is hopeful but unstable: people are celebrating freedom in Breachill while rebuilding homes, burying the dead, and bracing for reprisals.

## What Matters for Character Creation

- Your character should have a clear reason to support the Hellbreakers League, even if that reason is personal, conflicted, or pragmatic.
- You may be local to Isger or an outsider helping the cause. The adventure explicitly supports locals, Breachill survivors, foreign volunteers, veterans, exiles, and other rebels.
- Good campaign fits include characters who can navigate both combat and tense social situations. The adventure expects some mix of diplomacy, stealth, survival, and willingness to act in public-facing political situations.
- Goblin and hobgoblin characters are viable, but the setting assumes lingering tension and generational trauma from the Goblinblood Wars.
- The Player's Guide strongly encourages Hellbreaker-aligned builds, including the optional free archetype rule with the Hellbreaker archetype starting at 2nd level.
- Infernal or other unholy loyalties are a poor fit for this campaign. Undead ancestries are specifically not recommended.

## People and Places Worth Recognizing

- **Alceo Demetrias:** Cofounder of the Hellbreakers League and a cleric of Milani.
- **Phoebe Demetrias:** Cofounder of the Hellbreakers League and one of its leading commanders.
- **Abrogail Thrune II:** Ruler of Cheliax, whose influence hangs over the whole conflict.
- **Hedvend VI:** Steward of Isger, widely seen as more loyal to Cheliax than to the people he rules.
- **Breachill:** The town where the recent occupation and battle made the conflict impossible to ignore.
- **Citadel Altaerein:** The old Hellknight stronghold near Breachill that became part of the recent fighting.
- **Chitterwood:** The great forest in southern Isger, still deeply tied to the memory of the Goblinblood Wars and to present-day goblin communities.
- **Elidir:** Isger's capital, associated with wealth, government, and strong Chelish influence.
- **Saringallow:** A stubborn, independent-minded city and the current center of Hellbreaker activity.

## What the First Session Is Likely to Ask of You

- Be ready to explain how your character joined the Hellbreakers or why they chose to help now.
- Be ready to describe whether your character was in Breachill during the occupation, fought at Hellknight Hill, or arrived only after the battle.
- Be ready to tell a short story about your character's past, their values, or a hardship they survived. Chapter 1 opens with a scene that rewards exactly that kind of introduction.
- Be ready for travel under uncertain conditions. Outside Breachill, obvious revolutionary symbols can draw dangerous attention.

## Useful Questions to Answer Before Play

1. What did Chelish rule or the Goblinblood Wars cost you, your family, or your community?
2. Why did you join the Hellbreakers instead of staying neutral?
3. How do you feel about the Eagle Knights: liberators, meddlers, necessary allies, or something else?
4. How visible is your rebellion? Are you openly defiant, careful and covert, or still learning how dangerous this work is?
5. What tie do you already have to another PC, to Breachill, or to Saringallow?
