# Something may have happened to Gertraud at the warehouse — RESOLVED

!!! info "Field notes"

    **Raised:** Session 001
    **Resolved:** Session 002

The party suspected that Gertraud Mildenhall's disappearance from the Everbloom Imports warehouse was not a simple delay. This was borne out: in Session 002 you found her captured and held by Chelaxian loyalists who had taken over the warehouse. You killed them and freed her. She is alive and back at headquarters.
