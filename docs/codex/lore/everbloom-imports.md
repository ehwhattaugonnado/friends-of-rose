# Everbloom Imports

!!! info "Field notes"

    **Learned:** Session 001 (setup); Session 002 (visited; no supplies present)

A cover business operated by the Hellbreakers League at the docks in Saringallow, used to receive and store supplies shipped to the organization. You investigated it in Session 002 (see the [**Everbloom Imports (warehouse)**](../places/everbloom-imports.md) entry under Places for the layout and what happened there). The big revelation: the supply shipment the League was expecting was never actually there — Gertraud confirmed there was nothing for the League on the ship. Whether the crates were intercepted in transit or never loaded in the first place is unknown.
