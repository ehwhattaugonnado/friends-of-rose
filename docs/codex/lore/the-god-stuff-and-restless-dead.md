# The "God Stuff" and Restless Dead

!!! info "Field notes"

    **Learned:** Session 001

Utto mentioned that the dead have been restless in the region since "all that god stuff fell from the sky" — some kind of major divine event that seems to have disturbed the natural order. This appears to be a known local phenomenon, not just a rumor. The zombie giant bats you fought at the river crossing may be a manifestation of this.
