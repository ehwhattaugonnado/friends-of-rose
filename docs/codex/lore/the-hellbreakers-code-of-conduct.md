# The Hellbreakers' Code of Conduct

!!! info "Field notes"

    **Learned:** Session 002

Before your second mission, Rhokrag spelled out how the League wants you to operate. The work ahead involves lawbreaking — raids and piracy — but the rules are: take only what's needed and never from people who can't afford to lose it; the opposition is mostly hired guards and mercenaries doing a job, not enemies to be slaughtered; and above all, keep killing to a minimum. The League needs the people of Isger to see them as protectors, not warmongers — and Cheliax will seize on any bloodshed to brand the Hellbreakers as violent marauders. "No one will miss a bootlicker or two," but a trail of bodies is to be avoided. Defend yourselves, but don't give the enemy that propaganda.
