# The Warehouse Breach

!!! info "Field notes"

    **Learned:** Session 002

The Everbloom Imports warehouse was supposed to be a secret League location, but Chelaxian loyalists knew exactly where it was — they forced their way in, set a trap, and lay in wait. Neither Gertraud nor Rhokrag could explain how they found it. Rhokrag was clearly worried by the security implications. With no captives taken, the question of who tipped off the loyalists, and how, is wide open.
