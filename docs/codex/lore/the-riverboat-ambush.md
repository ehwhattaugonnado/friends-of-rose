# The Riverboat Ambush (next job)

!!! info "Field notes"

    **Learned:** Session 002

Rhokrag's next assignment for you, given at the end of Session 002. There's a spot up-river where the channel narrows past a rocky outcropping — an ideal place to ambush a passing ship. Cargo regularly moves from Elidir in the north down toward Cheliax in the south under the Chelaxian flag. The League's supplies are thin, and Rhokrag wants you to take one of these ships and bring it back to the warehouse. He gave the group ten gold to buy supplies in town before you set out. You haven't planned the ambush yet. See also: [**The Hellbreakers' Code of Conduct**](../lore/the-hellbreakers-code-of-conduct.md).
