# Zombie Giant Bats (Undead)

!!! info "Field notes"

    **Learned:** Session 001

The two massive bats that attacked you at the river crossing were undead, not living animals. Key things the party learned about them:
- Weak to vitality (healing) energy — Tainis's healing spells did double damage
- Resistant to mundane physical damage (slashing still worked but they were hardy)
- Immune or resistant to most mental and poison effects
- They have a void/rotting aura that deals void damage and can knock creatures unconscious
- Affected by the Slowed 1 condition — they could only take one action per turn
