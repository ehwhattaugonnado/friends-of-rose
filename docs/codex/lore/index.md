# Lore & Revelations

- ["Friends of Rose"](friends-of-rose.md) — The call-and-response used to enter the Hellbreakers' Saringallow headquarters: knock, say "we're some friends of Rose," and the reply is "any friend of Rose can find refuge here." Ethran told you this is the expected code, and you should use it if you ever need to bring someone else to the HQ.
- [Everbloom Imports](everbloom-imports.md) — A cover business operated by the Hellbreakers League at the docks in Saringallow, used to receive and store supplies shipped to the organization.
- [The "God Stuff" and Restless Dead](the-god-stuff-and-restless-dead.md) — Utto mentioned that the dead have been restless in the region since "all that god stuff fell from the sky" — some kind of major divine event that seems to have disturbed the natural order.
- [The Hellbreakers' Code of Conduct](the-hellbreakers-code-of-conduct.md) — Before your second mission, Rhokrag spelled out how the League wants you to operate.
- [The Riverboat Ambush (next job)](the-riverboat-ambush.md) — Rhokrag's next assignment for you, given at the end of Session 002.
- [The Warehouse Breach](the-warehouse-breach.md) — The Everbloom Imports warehouse was supposed to be a secret League location, but Chelaxian loyalists knew exactly where it was — they forced their way in, set a trap, and lay in wait.
- [Zombie Giant Bats (Undead)](zombie-giant-bats.md) — The two massive bats that attacked you at the river crossing were undead, not living animals.
