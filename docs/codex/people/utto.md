# Utto

!!! info "Field notes"

    **First appeared:** Session 001
    **Last seen:** Session 001 (the river crossing)
    **Status:** alive

Utto is the ferryman who operates the crossing on the North Conerica River. He saw the commotion from the water as he came back across and wasn't surprised to hear there had been trouble — he mentioned that the dead have been restless since "god stuff fell from the sky," and asked if you'd heard of a lich up on Lake Encarthan. He seemed like a local who pays attention to regional gossip but doesn't know much beyond rumors.
