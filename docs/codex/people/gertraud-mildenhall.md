# Gertraud Mildenhall

!!! info "Field notes"

    **First appeared:** Session 001 (mentioned by Rhokrag); met in Session 002
    **Last seen:** Session 002 (Saringallow HQ)
    **Status:** alive

Gertraud is a junior Hellbreakers smuggler and one of the League's trusted agents in Saringallow. She was assigned to meet an incoming supply vessel at the Everbloom Imports warehouse and inventory a shipment of weapons — but she never returned to headquarters, prompting Rhokrag to send you after her.

You found her at the warehouse, bound and gagged, with Chelaxian loyalists standing guard over her. She had come to unload the ship, found the loyalists already inside, and been captured. After you killed her captors, Yun Ze cut her free. She told you there were no supplies on the ship at all — the expected crates were simply never there — and she had no idea how the loyalists had learned the warehouse's location. Grateful for the rescue, she gave you two trinkets (the [**Whispering Wire**](../items/whispering-wire.md) and the [**Masquerade Scarf**](../items/masquerade-scarf.md)), helped you sort the loot, and returned with you to headquarters to report to Rhokrag.

She's petite, practical, and a little rattled but unhurt. See also: [**Everbloom Imports**](../places/everbloom-imports.md).
