# Xerra Bluebottle

!!! info "Field notes"

    **First appeared:** Session 001
    **Last seen:** Session 001 (Saringallow HQ)
    **Status:** alive

Xerra is the third of the three Saringallow Hellbreakers lieutenants. She's a halfling and the League's local baker and provisioner, responsible for keeping the organization fed and stocked. She interrupted Rhokrag's formal greeting with a tray of sticky buns. Her warmth seemed deliberate — the League clearly values having someone around who doesn't lead with intimidation.
