# Ethran Howell

!!! info "Field notes"

    **First appeared:** Session 001
    **Last seen:** Session 001 (arrived in Saringallow with you)
    **Status:** alive

You know Ethran from Session 0 in Breachill. He's a wounded Eagle Knight soldier and tactician who recruited the four of you into the Hellbreakers League and escorted you by wagon from Breachill to Saringallow. He's organized, pragmatic, and seems to have contacts along the road — he found you shelter in the barn at the unnamed village and paid out of his own pocket for rooms at the inn after the bat attack. He sheltered behind the cart during the fight at the river crossing, but afterwards gave the party a lesser bomb and a potency crystal from his own supplies.

Ethran is an Eagle Knight, a military order from Andoran that opposes Chelaxian expansion. His affiliation makes him a target for Chelaxian loyalists; the wanted posters you saw around Breachill included bounties on Eagle Knights and Andoran supporters.
