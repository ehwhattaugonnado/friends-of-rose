# Rhokrag

!!! info "Field notes"

    **First appeared:** Session 001
    **Last seen:** Session 002 (Saringallow HQ)
    **Status:** alive

Rhokrag is the stern hobgoblin lieutenant who handles mission assignments and military operations for the Hellbreakers in Saringallow. He was buried in documents when you arrived and sized up the new recruits quickly. He briefed you on the Hellbreakers League's history and current mission and assigned you your first job: find Gertraud Mildenhall and secure the Everbloom Imports supplies. He is direct, no-nonsense, and clearly runs the operational side of things.

After you rescued Gertraud, he was visibly troubled that the loyalists had found the supposedly secret warehouse and had no answer for how. The next morning he laid out the League's philosophy before handing you your next job: take only what's needed and never from those who can't afford it, win the people of Isger to the cause, and keep killing to a minimum so Cheliax can't paint the Hellbreakers as murderers. He admitted he gets emotional about the cause, then assigned you a river-piracy ambush and gave the group ten gold for supplies. See also: **The Riverboat Ambush (next job)**.
