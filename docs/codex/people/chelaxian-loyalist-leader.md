# Chelaxian Loyalist Leader

!!! info "Field notes"

    **First appeared:** Session 002
    **Last seen:** Session 002 (killed at the warehouse)
    **Status:** dead

The leader of the group holding the Everbloom Imports warehouse — a tall, dark-skinned man who stood guard over the captured Gertraud Mildenhall. He fought with a heavy crossbow, taking cover behind crates and critically wounding Edlo early in the fight. You killed him along with the rest of his group. You never learned his name, who he answered to, or how his people found the warehouse; no one was taken alive to question. You looted his heavy crossbow and bolts. See also: [**Chelaxian Loyalists**](../factions/chelaxian-loyalists.md), [**Everbloom Imports**](../places/everbloom-imports.md).
