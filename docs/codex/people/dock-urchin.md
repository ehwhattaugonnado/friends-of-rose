# Dock Urchin (unnamed)

!!! info "Field notes"

    **First appeared:** Session 002
    **Last seen:** Session 002 (the docks)
    **Status:** alive

A street-smart young halfling girl who hangs around the warehouse district watching everyone coming and going. Edlo picked her out of the crowd as someone with her finger on the pulse. She was wary of you at first but talked for coin — for four silver she told you that armed men had forced their way into the Everbloom Imports warehouse that morning without a key, that a "nice halfling lady" had gone in earlier with her key, and that no one had come out since. She offered further help if you need it and said she'll be around. You never got her name.
