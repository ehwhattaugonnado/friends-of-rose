# Maire Phelan

!!! info "Field notes"

    **First appeared:** Session 001
    **Last seen:** Session 002 (Saringallow HQ)
    **Status:** alive

Maire is one of the three lieutenants running the Hellbreakers' Saringallow headquarters. She greeted you warmly at the front door when you arrived — warm, friendly, the public face of the organization. Rhokrag called on her to bring you in. Her role within the League focuses on recruitment and public relations. After you returned from the warehouse, she thanked you, handed each of you two gold, and pointed you to the Fortnight Inn for the night.
