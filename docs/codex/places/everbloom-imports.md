# Everbloom Imports (warehouse)

!!! info "Field notes"

    **First visited/mentioned:** Session 001 (as your destination); visited Session 002

The Hellbreakers' cover warehouse on the eastern end of the Saringallow docks, used to receive and store supplies for the League. You visited it in Session 002. It's a minimalist, utilitarian warehouse — solid walls, a padlocked set of loading doors on the south side, and a smaller key-locked side door. You found that side door had been forced. Inside, just past the entrance, was a falling-crate tripwire trap that nearly killed Yun Ze. The main floor was an open space dotted with crates, where Chelaxian loyalists were holding Gertraud Mildenhall captive. You cleared the warehouse, freed Gertraud, and looted the dead. There was no shipment present — the supplies the League expected were never delivered, and whether the crates were intercepted in transit or never loaded in the first place is unknown. See also: [**Gertraud Mildenhall**](../people/gertraud-mildenhall.md), **Chelaxian Loyalist Leader**.
