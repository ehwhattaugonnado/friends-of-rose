# Saringallow — Hellbreakers Headquarters

!!! info "Field notes"

    **First visited:** Session 001

An unassuming two-story house in the southeastern part of Saringallow. The operational center is in a long basement with tables, maps of Isger on the walls, and stacks of documents. A corner has been cleared for a sparring area. Entry requires the call-and-response "friends of Rose" / "any friend of Rose can find refuge here." Rooms upstairs are available to League members while in town.
