# The Fortnight Inn

!!! info "Field notes"

    **First visited/mentioned:** Session 002

An inn in Saringallow that is friendly to the Hellbreakers, where Maire sent you to rest after the warehouse job. It's a lively nightlife spot with rooms above a bar — food, drink, and a resident five-piece band called the **Oasis Rain Showers** (who are genuinely good). You spent a night here recovering and laying low: wanted posters offering bounties for Isger/Chelaxian-loyalty targets are common in town, and Korrik kept his hobgoblin features concealed as usual.
