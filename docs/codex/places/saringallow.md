# Saringallow

!!! info "Field notes"

    **First visited:** Session 001

A town of around 2,500 people inside the walls, with another 800 or so outside. Saringallow is the Hellbreakers League's primary base of operations in Isger. Rhokrag described it as "largely a safe haven for revolutionaries," though he noted that Chelaxian loyalist numbers are growing and the organization can't assume that safety will last. The town is on the Conerica River and has active docks where the Hellbreakers maintain their cover warehouse.
