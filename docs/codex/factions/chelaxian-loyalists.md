# Chelaxian Loyalists

!!! info "Field notes"

    **First encountered:** Session 001 (unnamed village on Draymore Road); first fought Session 002

Cheliax is an empire that controls Isger as a vassal state. Chelaxian loyalists range from local true believers (like the four buzzed men who confronted your wagon in the unnamed village) to organized military forces. They pose an ongoing danger to the party and to the Hellbreakers. Wanted bounties were posted in Breachill targeting Andoran loyalists, Eagle Knights, and Chelaxian opponents. Rhokrag noted their numbers in Saringallow are growing.

In Session 002 you fought a squad of them for the first time: a leader and three others occupying the Everbloom Imports warehouse, where they had captured Gertraud Mildenhall and set a trap for intruders. They fought with crossbows, light maces, and morning stars, in leather armor. You killed all four. How they found the League's hidden warehouse is unknown, and because none survived, you couldn't learn who sent them. Rhokrag stressed that the League wants to avoid leaving "a trail of bodies," both morally and because Cheliax will use any killing to paint the Hellbreakers as violent marauders. See also: **Chelaxian Loyalist Leader**.
