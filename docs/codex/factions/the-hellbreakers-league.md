# The Hellbreakers League

!!! info "Field notes"

    **First encountered:** Session 001

A revolutionary organization dedicated to freeing Isger from Chelaxian rule. Founded by Alceo and Phoebe Demetrias, the League originally worked through peaceful economic resistance, but has shifted to active guerrilla operations. Saringallow is its primary headquarters; the League has a presence in multiple cities. Alceo and Phoebe handle the political side (including dealings with Hedvend the Steward of Isger), while Rhokrag leads the militaristic operations from Saringallow. The League maintains a network of safe houses, couriers, agents, and smugglers.

The local leadership in Saringallow consists of three lieutenants:
- [**Rhokrag**](../people/rhokrag.md) — missions and military operations
- [**Maire Phelan**](../people/maire-phelan.md) — recruitment and public relations
- [**Xerra Bluebottle**](../people/xerra-bluebottle.md) — provisioning and logistics
