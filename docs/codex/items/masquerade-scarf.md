# Masquerade Scarf

!!! info "Field notes"

    **Acquired:** Session 002
    **Held by:** Tainis Falco

A delicately embroidered scarf, given to you by Gertraud Mildenhall as thanks for the rescue. It's an invested magic item: once per day, you can spend a minute arranging it over your face to cast a 1st-rank Illusory Disguise, changing your appearance. (Item 2, worth ~30 gp.) Tainis took it.
