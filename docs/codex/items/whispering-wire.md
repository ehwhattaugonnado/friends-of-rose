# Whispering Wire

!!! info "Field notes"

    **Acquired:** Session 002
    **Held by:** Party stash

A coil of brightly colored wire, given to you by Gertraud Mildenhall as thanks for the rescue. It's a magic communication device: once every 10 minutes you can spend an action to silently mouth a short message into it, and the wire carries your words to a target you choose, as the Message cantrip. (Item 2, worth ~30 gp.) It went into the party stash; no one has claimed it specifically.
