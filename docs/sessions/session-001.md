# Session 001 — The Road to Saringallow

**Date:** 6/1/26

**Characters:** Edlo Zothus, Korrik Vesh, Tainis Falco, Yun Ze

**Duration:** ~1h 33m

**Locations:** Breachill (Session 0 recap), Unnamed roadside village (Draymore Road), River crossing on the North Conerica River, Inn across the river (near Mary Wim), Saringallow — Hellbreakers headquarters

**NPCs:** Ethran Howell, Brin, Utto, Maire Phelan, Rhokrag, Xerra Bluebottle, Cole Acton, Rabbit Dryden, Gertraud Mildenhall (missing)

> The party travels from Breachill to Saringallow with Ethran Howell, survives an undead bat ambush at the river crossing, joins the Hellbreakers, and accepts a mission to find the missing Gertraud Mildenhall at the Everbloom Imports warehouse.

## Session Events

### Recap and Reintroductions
- GM recapped the unrecorded Session 0: the party met Ethran Howell, an injured Eagle Knight recruiting for the Hellbreakers, in Breachill after the Battle of Hellknight Hill.
- Ethran arranged to take the party by wagon to Saringallow, a couple days' journey.
- Tainis Falco won a tavern storytelling contest the night before departure.
- Players reintroduced their characters: Edlo Zothus (human ranger, longbow, studded leather), Yun Ze (unarmed robed traveler, sleepy), Tainis Falco (bearded, oracle-flavored healer), Korrik Vesh (large hobgoblin, splint mail, massive polearm, covered face).

### The Unnamed Village
- The party stopped for the first night at a tiny roadside village on Draymore Road, outside Saringallow.
- Four buzzed Chelaxian loyalists from the local tavern confronted the wagon and told the outsiders to move on.
- Tainis defused the situation with Diplomacy, offering to buy the locals a round of drinks.
- The party drank with the loyalists and got through the evening without violence; Ethran secured a hayloft in a barn for the night.

### Morning on the Road
- Yun Ze was first awake, meditating in the barn doorway in the morning fog.
- Tainis observed Yun's meditation with quiet interest but did not start a conversation.
- The party traveled another full day and camped by the road.

### The River Crossing
- On the afternoon of the third day, the party reached a ferry crossing on the North Conerica River.
- Brin, the crossing attendant, told them the ferry had just left and would return in about an hour.
- At dusk, two massive bats approached from the north amid unnatural darkness; combat began.
- Recall Knowledge revealed the bats were undead — weak to vitality/healing energy, resistant to physical damage, and immune to most mental/poison effects.
- Korrik Vesh was dropped unconscious by a bat's void/rotting aura (Dying 1), prompting a rules walkthrough of dying, wounded, and hero points.
- Tainis kept Korrik alive with Nudge the Scales and a three-action Heal emanation; Korrik recovered and gained Wounded 1.
- Edlo landed three critical hits over the fight; the party destroyed both undead bats.
- Ethran, who had sheltered behind the cart, emerged and gave the party a lesser bomb and a potency crystal for the stash.
- Edlo recovered six of the seven arrows he had fired.

### Skipping Rocks at the Crossing
- The party cleaned up at the river while waiting for the ferry.
- Ferryman Utto arrived and asked what had happened after seeing trouble in the sky.
- Utto said the dead have been restless since "all that god stuff fell from the sky" and mentioned a rumored lich on Lake Encarthan.
- Society checks failed — no one knew anything more about the lich rumor.
- Ethran paid out of pocket for a proper room at the inn across the river (near Mary Wim) for the night.

### Saringallow and the Hellbreakers Headquarters
- The party arrived at Saringallow (~2,500 in town, ~800 beyond the walls) the next morning.
- Ethran led them to the Hellbreakers' headquarters, an unassuming two-story house in the southeastern part of town.
- The party learned the "friends of Rose" call-and-response used to enter.
- Maire Phelan, a warm recruitment/PR lieutenant, greeted them and led them to a basement command room full of maps of Isger, documents, and a sparring area.
- They met Rhokrag, a stern hobgoblin lieutenant, and Xerra Bluebottle, a halfling baker who brought sticky buns; recruits Cole Acton and Rabbit Dryden were sparring nearby.
- Rhokrag explained the Hellbreakers League — founded by Alceo and Phoebe Demetrias — has shifted from peaceful resistance to guerrilla revolution aiming to liberate Isger from Cheliax.
- Rhokrag assigned the party's first mission: investigate the Hellbreakers' dockside warehouse (cover business Everbloom Imports), find the missing Gertraud Mildenhall, and keep the supplies from Logas intact.
- The session ended with the party accepting the mission, set to head to the docks next session.

## Mechanics and Rules Notes

- Initiative in Pathfinder 2e is rolled with Perception by default.
- If the party notices enemies before combat, weapons can be assumed drawn at the start rather than costing an action.
- Recall Knowledge was introduced as a useful combat action for identifying enemies and weaknesses.
- Stepping (5 ft) does not trigger reactions such as Reactive Strike.
- Multiple spells can be cast in one turn if the actions are available; Heal is one action when used within touch range.
- Hunt Prey reduces the ranger's multiple-attack penalty (−3/−6 instead of −5/−10).
- Dying/Wounded explained: each hit while down raises Dying toward death (4–5); being healed up leaves a Wounded level that raises future starting Dying.
- Hero points: one at session start plus one per hour; can be spent to reroll or to shed Dying and stabilize at 0 HP.
- Battle Medicine and Treat Wounds work on dying creatures but require touch and a healer's kit.

## Decisions and Outcomes

- The party traveled with Ethran Howell from Breachill to Saringallow.
- The party de-escalated the Chelaxian loyalist confrontation in the village rather than fight.
- The party defeated two undead bats at the river crossing; Korrik was downed and revived.
- The party reached Saringallow and made contact with the Hellbreakers headquarters and its three lieutenants.
- The party accepted Rhokrag's mission to investigate Everbloom Imports.

## Unresolved Threads

- Gertraud Mildenhall is missing after going to inventory supplies at Everbloom Imports.
- The Hellbreakers' dockside supplies from Logas may be threatened or compromised.
- Chelaxian loyalist presence in Saringallow is growing.
- Undead activity near Saringallow appears tied to divine debris ("god stuff") that fell from the sky.
- A rumored lich on Lake Encarthan was mentioned but is unverified.
- Tainis Falco's build may be adjusted (possibly toward bard) before or during early play.

## Player Knowledge

- Ethran Howell is an injured Eagle Knight who recruited the party into the Hellbreakers.
- The Hellbreakers League, founded by Alceo and Phoebe Demetrias, is a revolutionary group now running active guerrilla operations to free Isger from Cheliax.
- The Saringallow headquarters is an open secret entered via the "friends of Rose" password; its three local leaders are Maire Phelan, Rhokrag, and Xerra Bluebottle.
- Everbloom Imports is a Hellbreakers cover business and dockside warehouse.
- Gertraud Mildenhall is a Hellbreakers smuggler who failed to return from the warehouse.
- The dead have been restless regionally since "god stuff" fell from the sky; a lich on Lake Encarthan is rumored but unconfirmed.
- The party must investigate the warehouse immediately and try to preserve both Gertraud and the supplies.
